FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is #100DaysOfCode challenge?

    The 100 Days Of Code challenge encouraging beginners to start to learn code, encourage them to post a tweet about what they did and use the hashtag of #100DaysOfCode and submit the final project after the event into showcase-your-project repository.

  • Is it required to post a tweet everyday?

    You need to tweet your progress everyday with the #100DaysOfCode hashtag, but you are allowed to skip a day, your health is important. You can see the tweet example here: https://twitter.com/search?q=%23100DaysOfCode&src=typeahead_click

  • Who to join?

    Only Filipino students & career shifters can participate to this event.

  • Is there any swag we can get even we don't win?

    We will try to give some swags to participants but this is only limited and it takes time to deliver. Even if you don't win, the most important thing is that you learn, improve your coding skills and etc.

  • What are the prizes for the winners?

    We will choose the top 5 awesome projects that will be submitted to showcase-your-project repository. The 5 winners will get T-shirt codes and some SWAG ^_^

  • Will I get certificate if I join?

    Yes, you will receive a certificate of participation if you complete the #100DaysOfCode challenge
